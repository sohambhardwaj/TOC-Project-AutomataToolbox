Automata Toolbox
NFA2DFA RegEx2DFA About

‘Automata Toolbox’ can perform various operations on Regular Expressions, Non-deterministic Finite Automata (NFA) and Deterministic Finite Automata (DFA). You can convert a NFA to its equivalent DFA or we can convert a Regular Expression to its equivalent DFA. Along with the conversions, visuals have been provided at each step to give the user a better understanding. It has an easy to user interface with full flexibility and instructions given to the user.
